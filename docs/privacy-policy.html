Med.IA — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

Med.IA ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered medical education application designed for medical students to practice diagnostic skills. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Conversation Data

When you use the app to practice patient interviews, your text inputs are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate AI responses. These conversations are processed in real time and are not stored on any external server by us.

Local Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Session progress and statistics (streaks, completed cases)
  • App preferences and settings (language, theme)
  • Feedback and ratings you provide on AI responses

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • We do not require user accounts or login
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use advertising or ad tracking

Third-Party Services

The app uses Anthropic's Claude API to power AI-generated patient conversations. Text you enter during interviews is sent to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using the app's local database. We do not operate external servers or databases that store your personal data. Communication with the Claude API is encrypted via HTTPS.

Data Export

The app allows you to export feedback data via the iOS share sheet. This is entirely optional and initiated only by you.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for medical students and professionals. It is not intended for use by children under the age of 17.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address provided in App Store Connect.
